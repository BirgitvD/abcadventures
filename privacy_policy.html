ABC Adventures (“the App”) is developed and published by Birgit van Driel (“we”, “our”, or “us”).

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in the App. Please read it carefully. If you have questions, contact us at abcadventures.app@gmail.com.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • The App does not require registration or login.
  • The App does not collect names, email addresses, or location data.
  • The App does not access the device camera, microphone, or contacts.
  • The App does not track users or show advertisements.

2. Offline Use

The App works entirely offline. All content, including sounds and images, is stored locally on your device. No internet connection is required or used while playing.

3. Data Shared with Third Parties

We do not share any user data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is designed specifically for young children (ages 1–5). We follow best practices to ensure a safe experience:

  • No third-party ads
  • No external links leading outside the app
  • No in-app purchases

Parents can allow their children to use the app without concern for online exposure or data tracking.

5. Permissions

The App may request minimal permissions necessary to play sounds stored within the app. These permissions are used solely for app functionality and never to access personal data.

6. Technology & Frameworks

The App is built with modern Android technologies, including:

  • Kotlin and Jetpack Compose
  • Hilt for dependency injection
  • Android Jetpack libraries (ViewModel, lifecycle)
  • MediaPlayer (Android SDK) for local audio playback

All sound and image assets are stored locally on your device and not uploaded or shared externally.

7. Data Retention & Security

Because the App does not collect personal data, no user records are stored on our servers. If you contact us by email, we may retain your message temporarily for support purposes and delete it once your inquiry is resolved.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with a new “Last updated” date. Material updates (such as adding analytics or online features) will be clearly indicated.
